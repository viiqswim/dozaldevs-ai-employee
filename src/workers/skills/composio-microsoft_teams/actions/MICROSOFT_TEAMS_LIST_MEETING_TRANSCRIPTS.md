# MICROSOFT_TEAMS_LIST_MEETING_TRANSCRIPTS

**Description**: List meeting transcripts for a Microsoft Teams online meeting. Retrieves all transcripts associated with a specific meeting, including metadata such as creation time, meeting organizer, and content URL. Use this action when you need to access or review the transcription records of a meeting, for example to extract meeting notes, analyze discussion topics, or maintain compliance records.
