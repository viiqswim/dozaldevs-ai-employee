# MICROSOFT_TEAMS_GET_TEAM_OPERATION

**Description**: Tool to retrieve the status of a Teams async operation using teamId and operationId. Use when you need to poll and track the progress of long-running operations like team creation or archiving. Microsoft recommends waiting at least 30 seconds between polling requests.
