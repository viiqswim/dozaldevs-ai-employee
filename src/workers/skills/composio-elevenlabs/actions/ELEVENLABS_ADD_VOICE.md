# ELEVENLABS_ADD_VOICE

**Description**: Adds a custom voice by uploading audio samples for voice cloning. Requires a name and at least one audio file. Recommended: 1-2 minutes of clear audio without background noise. Supported formats: mp3, wav, ogg. Returns a voice_id that can be used for text-to-speech after processing completes.
