# INSTAGRAM_GET_POST_INSIGHTS

**Description**: DEPRECATED: Use INSTAGRAM_GET_IG_MEDIA_INSIGHTS instead. Get Instagram post insights/analytics (impressions, reach, engagement, etc.). Requires a Business or Creator account; personal accounts cannot access insights. Metrics may be unavailable for several minutes after publishing; verify post status is FINISHED before calling.
