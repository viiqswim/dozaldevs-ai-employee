# QUICKBOOKS_CAPTURE_CHARGE

**Description**: Tool to capture funds for an existing charge that was authorized but not captured. Use when you need to complete a previously authorized payment transaction in QuickBooks Payments API. This action finalizes the charge and transfers the funds from the customer's payment method.
