# QUICKBOOKS_GET_REPORT_CUSTOMER_SALES

**Description**: Generate a customer sales report in QuickBooks showing sales transactions and totals for customers. Can filter by specific customers, date ranges, and accounting method. Returns a hierarchical report with customer names, IDs, and their sales amounts.
