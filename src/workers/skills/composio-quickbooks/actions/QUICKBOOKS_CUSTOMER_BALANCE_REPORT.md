# QUICKBOOKS_CUSTOMER_BALANCE_REPORT

**Description**: Generate a customer balance report in QuickBooks showing outstanding balances for customers. Can filter by specific customers, date ranges, payment status, and accounting method. Returns a hierarchical report with customer names, IDs, and their current balance amounts.
