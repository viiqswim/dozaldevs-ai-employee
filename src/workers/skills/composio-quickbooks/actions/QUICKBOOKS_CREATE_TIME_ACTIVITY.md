# QUICKBOOKS_CREATE_TIME_ACTIVITY

**Description**: Create a new time activity record in QuickBooks Online. TimeActivity tracks employee or vendor time worked on jobs or projects. Use when recording billable or non-billable hours for employees or vendors.
