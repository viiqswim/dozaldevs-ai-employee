# QUICKBOOKS_CREATE_ESTIMATE

**Description**: Creates a new estimate in QuickBooks Online. An estimate represents a proposal for goods or services that a customer may purchase. Use this to provide pricing quotes to customers before they commit to a purchase. This action requires a valid customer ID and at least one line item with item details and amount. The created estimate can later be converted to an invoice when the customer accepts the proposal.
