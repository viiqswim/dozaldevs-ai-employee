# QUICKBOOKS_CREATE_PAYMENT

**Description**: Creates a payment record in QuickBooks Online. The Payment entity records payment from customers against single or multiple invoices and credit memos. Use this action when recording customer payments, whether full payment of an invoice or partial payment across multiple invoices.
