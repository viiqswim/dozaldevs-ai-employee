# QUICKBOOKS_CREATE_JOURNAL_ENTRY

**Description**: Tool to create a new journal entry in QuickBooks Online. Use when you need to make adjusting or correcting entries that directly affect account balances. Journal entries require at least two lines with total debits equaling total credits.
